Geometry



Before we move further, let's look at what geometry is all about


Geometry Definition


Geometry is the study of different types of shapes, figures and sizes in Maths or in real life. The study of different types of shapes has to do with the area, perimeter, volume, surface area, etc of the shapes.


Under geometry, we have essential and commonly used mathematical formulas. Below are the list of them.


Formulas For Area


  • Rectangle ==> length × width

  • Triangle ==> 1/2(base × height)

  • Circle ==> 𝛑r^2, where r is the radius.

  • Trapezoid ==> 1/2(a + b)×height, where a and b are the bases.

Formulas For Perimeter


  • Rectangle ==> 2×(length + width)

  • Square ==> 4l , where l is the length

  • Triangle ==> (a + b + c), where (a, b, c) are the three sides

  • Circumference of a Circle ==> 2𝛑r

Formulas For Volume


  • Cube ==> l^3, where l is the side length.

  • Rectangular Prism ==> length × width × height

  • Sphere ==> 4/3𝛑r^3

  • Cone ==> 1/3𝛑r^2×h

Formulas For Surface Area


  • Cube ==> 6s^2

  • Cylinder ==> 2𝛑r(r+h)

  • Sphere ==> 4𝛑r^2

Pythagorean Theorem


  • For a right triangle ==> a^2 + b^2 = c^2, where c is the hypotenuse.
